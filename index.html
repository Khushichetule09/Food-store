About Us

Welcome to our Happy Da Dhaba, your go-to destination for fast, fresh, and flavorful meals on the go. We’re more than just a food counter — we’re a passionate team serving up quality ingredients, quick bites, and delicious moments every day. Whether you're grabbing lunch between meetings, picking up a snack on the run, or craving comfort food at the end of a long day, our counter is here to satisfy. From signature sandwiches and sizzling street food to healthy bowls and refreshing beverages, there’s something for every craving. At Happy Da Dhaba, we believe good food should be convenient, affordable, and always made with care. Stop by and taste the difference!
